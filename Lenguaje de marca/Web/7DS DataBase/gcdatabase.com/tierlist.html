Character Tierlist (updated as of 13/12/2021)

This tierlist ranks different characters into 5 ranks: SS, S, A, B, C.
Special thanks to everyone who helped out - including top 100 and Challenger PvP players for advising on the tierlist.
For invididual character breakdown, please look into the specific character pages.
Note: The rating of the unit is derived by taking commonly team composition into consideration.
For PvP, the rating is a combination of both geared and ungeared (detailed breakdown in character page).
For farming, the following aspects are taken into consideration: boss stages, gear stages, food stages.
For passive, the character's viability on both main team and subslot are taken into consideration.
