Status Effect List

The categories Buff, Debuff and Eternal Effects consider units' skills, Ultimate Moves and passives but not Commandments, which are listed separately.

Buff Effects

Increase Stats Effects

Name Icon Description Users
Increase Attack Increases the Attack of the target(s).
Increase Attack-related stats Increases the Attack, Pierce Rate, Critical Chance, and Critical Damage.
Increase Defense Increases the Defense of the target(s).
Increase Defense-related stats Increases the Defense, Resistance, Critical Resist, and Critical Defense of the target(s).
Increase HP-related stats Increases the Recovery Rate, Regeneration Rate, Lifesteal, and HP of the target(s).
Solidify Increases target's resistance by 30% for 3 turns. Stackable up to 3 times per unit.

Protection Buff Effects

Name Icon Description Users
Barrier Absorbs a certain amount of damage taken.
Evasion Negates incoming damage and gains immunity to debuffs.
Immunity Removes debuffs and gains immunity to debuffs.
Petrification Immunity Becomes immune to Petrify.
Taunt Directs all enemies' single target attacks to the caster.

Damaging Buff Effects

Name Icon Description Users
Abyss Inflicts 30% of total damage dealt this turn on all enemies at the start of the enemies' turn(s).
Reflect Reflects a percentage of the damage taken back at the attacker.

Special Buff Effects

Name Icon Description Users
Resurrect Revives an ally that dies and fix its HP to 1.
Remove Ultimate Move Gauge Removes all Ultimate Move Gauge Orbs from attacker's Ultimate Move Gauge.

Debuff Effects

Decrease Stats Effects

Name Icon Description Users

Decrease Critical Chance Decreases the Critical Chance of the target(s).

Decrease Defense-related stats Decreases the Defense, Resistance, Critical Resistance and Critical Defense of the target(s).
Decrease Critical Defense-related stats Decreases the Critical Defense and Critical Resistance of the target(s).
Decrease HP Decreases the HP of the target(s).
Decrease HP-related stats Decreases the Recovery Rate, Regeneration Rate, Lifesteal, and HP of the target(s).
Decrease Ultimate Move damage Decreases the damage taken from Ultimate Move(s) of the target(s).

Damage-over-Time Effects

Name Icon Description Users
Bleed Deals 90% of the damage dealt as additional damage at the end of every turn to the target(s).

Incapacitation Debuff Effects

Name Icon Description Users
Freeze Removes all buffs, debuffs and stances from the target, which will not be able to use any skills. Breaks if target takes damage.
Petrify Removes all buffs, debuffs and stances from the target, which will not be able to use any skills. Breaks if target takes damage.
Stun Target(s) cannot use any skills.

Special Debuff Effects

Name Icon Description Users

Disable Ultimate Move Gauge Target(s) will not be able to gain Ultimate Move Gauges.
Ignite Increases damage taken by 10%. Stackable.
Infect Nullifies any recovery, regeneration and heal of the target(s).
Inhale Decreases the enemy's max HP by a certain amount, and recovers this unit's HP by the amount decreased for 1 turn.
Increase damage taken Increases damage taken by the target(s).

Eternal Effects

These effects cannot be removed by ordinary means. The only way for these effects to cease applying is for the caster to die, the effect to expire or a set condition to be met.

Eternal User Effects

Name Icon Description Users
Charm The attacks of the charmed target will be negated if the attack would deal damage to the caster (including AoE skills).
Coerce The target can only target the caster and cannot activate Debuff skills.
Transform The battle state of the unit changes. Becomes immune to Freeze, Petrify and Stun. Recovers HP to max.
Increase Attack-related stats Increases the Attack, Pierce Rate, Critical Chance, and Critical Damage.
Decrease Defense-related stats Decreases the Defense, Resistance, Critical Resist, and Critical Defense of the target(s).
Incapacitation Immunity Becomes immune to Freeze, Petrify and Stun.
Decrease Attack-related stats Decrease the Attack, Pierce Rate, Critical Rate, Critical Damage of the target(s).
Decrease HP-related stats Decreases the Recovery Rate, Regeneration Rate, Lifesteal, and HP of the target(s).

Eternal Boss Effects

Name Icon Description Users

These effects are active as long as the caster is on the field. They apply for both allies and enemies, including the caster. If multiple allies posses a Commandment, only the effect of the leftmost alive ally is active. These effects are not active in Deathmatches.

Name Icon Description Buff Icon Users PvP-only

Commandment of Repose Units who would use 2 or more skills in a single turn will have their Attack-related stats decreased by 30% before they use those skills. Yes
Commandment of Patience Apply a Solidify buff on a unit every time that unit receives damage from skills. Yes
Commandment of Selflessness ? ? ? ?

Commandment of Purity Decreases the damage of ranged skills by 35%, excluding Ultimate Moves. - Yes
Commandment of Love Decreases Attack-related stats by 20% for 3 turns upon damaging an enemy with a skill. No
Commandment of Piety Removes debuffs from a unit that moved skills and depletes the Ultimate Move Gauge of that unit by 1. - No
